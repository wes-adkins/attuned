Why Attuned exists

It's pretty simple, actually. Attuned gives Pastoral clarity and actionable items from Planning Center data—so your team knows who needs care, follow-up, and encouragement this week.

Planning Center is excellent at collecting and reporting church data. But most church teams don’t struggle with access to information—they struggle with interpretation at scale.

Attuned exists to bridge that gap. It translates engagement data into pastoral priorities that fit naturally into weekly staff rhythms, so care doesn’t depend on who remembers to run which report.

Planning Center reports. Attuned interprets.

Attuned doesn’t replace Planning Center—it makes its data pastorally usable.

Planning Center reporting
  • Shows what happened
  • Requires manual setup and filtering
  • Designed for administrators and power users
  • Leaves interpretation and follow-up to staff capacity
Attuned actionable data
  • Highlights who needs attention now
  • Interprets patterns automatically
  • Designed for pastors, execs, and care teams
  • Creates consistent follow-up across the church
  • Surfaces formation-related patterns over time
